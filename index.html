Our Story

Built on a Mission,
Not Just a Business

Paul Willison's path into roofing was personal. After watching unreliable contractors exploit homeowners after storm damage — leaving incomplete work, inflated bills, and families fighting their insurance companies alone — he decided to do it differently.

Roof Rescue 911 was built on a simple belief: every homeowner and business deserves a contractor they can trust, a process they can understand, and a roof that actually protects them.

Paul brings hands-on construction experience, deep knowledge of the insurance claim process, and a personal commitment to every job. He doesn't send a crew and disappear — he's there.
